About Us


Lethukuphila Foundation is a compassionate and dedicated non-profit organization that stands as a beacon of hope for disabled children in South Africa. Our mission is to uplift, empower, and transform the lives of these young individuals, who often face challenges related to disabilities and limited access to fundamental care.

Driven by a commitment to inclusivity and a vision of a more promising future, Lethukuphila Foundation works tirelessly to bridge the gaps and disparities that hinder the potential of disabled children. Our organization is more than just a support system; it's a community of caring individuals, volunteers, and partners who believe in the inherent worth of every child.

Through innovative programs, comprehensive care, and unwavering dedication, we create an environment where disabled children can thrive, learn, and develop their unique strengths. Our focus extends beyond medical care; we aim to foster a sense of belonging, resilience, and empowerment in every child we serve.

Lethukuphila Foundation envisions a South Africa where disabled children are not only embraced with empathy but also celebrated for their abilities. We advocate for policy changes, raise social awareness, and collaborate with stakeholders to create a more inclusive society that ensures equal opportunities for all children.

Join us in our journey to create a brighter future for disabled children. Together, we can make a lasting impact, one life at a time.

Our Mission

"Empowering Dreams, Transforming Lives: Lethukuphila Foundation is dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of disabled children in South Africa. Our mission is to provide unwavering support, comprehensive care, and equal opportunities to these children, enabling them to thrive and reach their full potential. Through compassionate outreach, innovative programs, and a commitment to inclusivity, we aspire to uplift the spirits of these young individuals and create a more inclusive and promising future for all."

Our Vision

"Envisioning Equality, Igniting Possibilities: Lethukuphila Foundation strives to become a leading force in advocating for the rights and well-being of disabled children in South Africa. Our vision is to create a society where every disabled child is embraced with empathy, empowered with opportunities, and celebrated for their unique abilities. Through collaborative efforts, innovative initiatives, and a steadfast commitment to change, we aim to catalyze a transformative shift in perception, policy, and practice, fostering an inclusive and supportive environment for all children to thrive."

Our Values

  • Empathy: We deeply understand and empathize with the challenges disabled children face, fostering a compassionate and supportive environment.
  • Inclusivity: We believe in the inherent worth of every child, advocating for an inclusive society where all children have equal access to opportunities.
  • Empowerment: We empower disabled children to discover and develop their unique strengths, enabling them to achieve their fullest potential.
  • Collaboration: We collaborate with communities, stakeholders, and partners to create a collective impact and drive meaningful change.
